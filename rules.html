Blackjack Rules

  • You have to pay 10$ to start a game.
  • To start the game please tap on button "Start Game".
  • You can take new card until you have Blackjack (i. e. sum = 21) or your sum exceed 21.
  • You can stop taking new cards and take a prize only if your sum >= 15
  • Prize list:
    1. Sum = 15, Prize = 10$
    2. Sum = 16, Prize = 11$
    3. Sum = 17, Prize = 12$
    4. Sum = 18, Prize = 13$
    5. Sum = 19, Prize = 14$
    6. Sum = 20, Prize = 15$
    7. Blackjack, Prize = 30$
